Wiltune

Project Info:

As part of my work on Wiltune, a game project, I was responsible for developing the enemy AI system. I used a decision tree approach to implement enemy behavior, which involved analyzing the game design documents and iterating on the AI until it provided a compelling gameplay experience. Overall, this project allowed me to develop my skills in game AI and programming, and I'm proud of the results that my team and I were able to achieve.
